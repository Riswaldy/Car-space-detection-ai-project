Parking Space Detection

With the rapid increase in the number of vehicles, parking has become a major issue in many malls and tourist destinations. Searching for available parking can take time, contributing to traffic congestion. Here, I propose a modern solution to this problem by incorporating Artificial Intelligence (AI) into the parking system. Parking managers can help motorists identify available parking spaces. This project intends to use AI to speed up the parking procedure, minimize congestion, and improve the parking system's efficiency.
